Educational travel programs for Alumni & Friends

About us

Alumni Western’s Discover the World travel program continues to visit some of the most remarkable places in the world. The 2018 offerings include several new trips and a good number of our favourite destinations.

Join like-minded travellers on one of our journeys, enhanced by knowledgeable lecturers and tour directors, offering an exceptional educational experience.

Discover the World trips are open to all Western alumni, their families and friends, current and retired staff and faculty and their families and other members of the Western community.

Our many repeat travellers are testimony to the quality and variety of our adventures.

Activity levels

Good health is recommended, as you will be walking most days 1 to 2 hours at a comfortable pace on a relatively flat terrain, but sometimes on cobblestones or other uneven surfaces.

Moderate
Good health is required as you will be walking most days for 2 to 4 hours at a comfortable pace on a variety of terrains including small hills and cobblestones or other uneven surfaces.

Moderately Active
These trips have more activity than the others and can be considered “soft adventure.” There is more walking and climbing and you will need to be physically prepared for this.

Please Note:

All our programs require that you are physically independent and mobile. If you do require assistance, you should travel with a companion. Most of our hotels and ships are not wheelchair accessible. Please do enquire before booking.
